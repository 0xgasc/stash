## üí° Alternative Model: "Digital Vault" Subscription

Instead of selling storage, sell **peace of mind:**

### The Vault Model

**Monthly Plans:**
- **Vault Starter**: $9/mo ‚Üí 10GB/year permanent storage allowance
- **Vault Pro**: $29/mo ‚Üí 50GB/year permanent storage allowance
- **Vault Business**: $99/mo ‚Üí 250GB/year permanent storage allowance

**How it works:**
1. Customer pays monthly subscription
2. Each month, they get X GB "storage credits"
3. Upload anytime during subscription
4. Files remain **permanent forever**, even if subscription ends
5. Unlike Google Drive, **you own your storage allocation**

**Example:**
- User pays $9/mo for 12 months = $108
- They get 10GB permanent storage (cost us $0.50)
- Margin: **$107.50** (21,500%)
- Even if they churn after 1 year, files stay permanent
- We keep the margin, they keep their data

**Why customers love this:**
- Familiar subscription model (like Netflix)
- "Set it and forget it" backup
- Even if they cancel, files are safe
- Psychological: paying $9/mo feels better than $99 upfront

## ü§î The Big Question: Which Model?

### Option A: PAYG ($0.01/MB)
- **Pros:** Simple, transparent, matches Arweave ethos
- **Cons:** Low ARPU, price-sensitive customers
- **Best for:** Developer/API users

### Option B: Tiered Annual ($99-999/year)
- **Pros:** Predictable revenue, higher ARPU
- **Cons:** Upfront payment barrier
- **Best for:** Businesses, creators

### Option C: Monthly Vault ($9-99/mo)
- **Pros:** Low friction, familiar, best LTV
- **Cons:** Higher churn risk
- **Best for:** Consumers, mass market

### üèÖ **Recommendation: Hybrid Model**

**Offer all three:**
1. **Free tier** (500MB, 1 month) ‚Üí acquisition
2. **Monthly Vault** ($9-99/mo) ‚Üí consumers
3. **Annual Plans** ($99-999/year) ‚Üí creators/biz (10% discount)
4. **PAYG API** ($0.01/MB) ‚Üí developers

**Why:** Capture ALL segments, maximize revenue, reduce risk
